Services

We do lots of cool things

  • Topology Analyze

    Explore detailed pangenome graph statistics and analysis on the dedicated results page, which includes various panels highlighting key aspects of the graph.

    Learn More
  • Structural Variation Discover

    Identify structural variations in the pangenome by analyzing the graph topology.

    Learn More
  • Multi-GFA Compare

    Select multiple GFA files and their associated graph models to combine and visualize on a single page, which allows for side-by-side comparisons and better contextual analysis of structural variations.

    Learn More
